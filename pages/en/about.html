My Journey

From Fundamental Physics...

With a Ph.D. in Physics, I spent several years designing complex models and analyzing massive datasets to understand the fundamental laws of nature. Scientific research instilled in me a relentless rigor: the ability to isolate signal from noise, test hypotheses through experimentation, and never settle for an approximation when precision is critical.

...to Data Engineering

Today, I apply this same analytical rigor to the challenges of the corporate world as a Data Engineer Consultant. I don't just connect pipes; I build resilient, scalable, and auditable data infrastructures.

My role is to translate your business challenges (cost optimization, risk forecasting, automation) into cutting-edge technical architectures (Cloud, Data Warehouse, ELT pipelines).

My philosophy in 3 points:

  • Quality first: Incorrect data is more dangerous than no data.
  • Smart automation: If a task must be done twice, it must be automated (CI/CD, IaC).
  • ROI Focus: Technology is a means, the ultimate goal is business value.
